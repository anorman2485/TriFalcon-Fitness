## 5. Logging a Workout

This feature allows you to record your fitness activity.

### Steps:
1. From the Dashboard, click "Log Workout"  
2. Fill out the form  

### Activity Information
- Activity Type:
  - Strength Training  
  - Cardio / Endurance  
  - Flexibility  
  - Sport  

- Exercise Name  
  - Example: Bench Press, Running  

### Strength Section (if applicable)
- Weight Lifted (lbs)  
- Sets  
- Reps  

### Cardio Section (if applicable)
- Distance (miles)  
- Duration (minutes)  

3. Click "Save Workout"  

[Insert screenshot of workout form]

### Result:
- Your workout is saved  
- It will be used in your progress charts

[Insert screenshot of analytics page]

### What You Will See:

#### Strength Progression

#### Cardio Milestones

## 7. Troubleshooting

### Cannot Log In
- Ensure email and password are correct  
- Try resetting your password  

### Workout Not Saving
- Make sure all required fields are filled  
- Check that numeric fields are valid  

### No Data in Charts
- You must log workouts first  
- Charts only display recorded data  

---

## Tips for Best Use

- Log workouts consistently for accurate tracking  
- Use clear exercise names (e.g., "Bench Press")  
- Check your progress regularly
